Notes of the webinar on how to select a good research topic and get it published

Category: Interesting things I found

A research project can be divided into several steps: Topic -> Model -> Data -> Findings -> Writing and Motivation. A good model should be clean and simplify all other irrelevant parts. The quality, granularity and credibility of data are the most important features of the data.

The trend of topics of top journals

  • Research on management comes from the practice but is a step further from the practice.
  • So, it should have implications on the real world and the hot topic follows the evolution of the technologies and trends
    • Ten years ago, researches were studying e-commerce and social media
    • Seven or eight years ago, omni-channel retailing
    • Later, mobile internet marketing
    • Sharing economics, Didi and Uber
    • AI, algorithmic pricing, algorithmic recommendation, AI customer service
  • On one hand, top journals prefer the latest topics
  • On the other hand, the hot topics are competing and it is difficult to distinguish the study from other papers

Tips

  • For empirical researches, sometimes it is important to start from the data and then decide on the topic
  • Researchers should well position the paper in the literature through the comparison with other literature
  • A good topic should be
    • Important to the real world and theory
    • Understudied or unstudied
  • When selecting a research topic, it is important to combine the existing literature and insights of the industry
  • Have a thorough grasp of classical researches and pay attention to the cut-edging researches and latest business practice
  • We can learn a lot from the classical researches, like how to refine ideas and how to process data
  • Focus on two or three topics at the stage of PhD and master to get a thorough understanding of these topics

Common mistakes in selecting a topic

  • Only an overall topic but no detailed ideas
  • Overemphasized the coverage but overlooked the depth. Depth is much more important than coverage
  • Only seek research ideas in the literature. It can help researchers find understudied questions, but it is not that important
  • Simply combing two models. The combined model may not be better than two separated models.
